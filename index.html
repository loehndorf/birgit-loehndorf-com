About Me

I’m a strategy-driven entrepreneur and former academic in the field of marketing and management. With over a decade of experience in innovation, digital transformation, and business development, I co-founded and led Quantego, a deep tech company in energy optimization. The venture secured competitive funding, went through international accelerators, and landed major clients like BASF and Verbund, culminating in a successful exit in 2024. Today, I operate as an independent advisor—supporting organizations across industries in building digital strategies, scaling business models, and implementing automation-driven communication and marketing structures. My work ranges from start-ups and scale-ups to traditional businesses navigating digital transformation.
